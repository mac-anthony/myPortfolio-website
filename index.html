About Me

Hi, My name is Okeke Mac-anthony and I am a freelance web designer who specializes in creating beautiful and dynamic web pages and applications.i have been in the field for 2 and half years,and have been enjoying every minute of it. My interests span across all aspects of design and development. I am a Tech enthusiast, Front-end designer, developer, and a hard working fellow who is always eager to learn and improve. Check out some of the links below to see what I've been up to lately.

More About Me

I was born in Aba, Abia State. but now living in Awka, Anambra State. I graduated from the department of Computer Science in Nnamdi Azikiwe University.
I was opportuned to work as an Intern at LearnFactory (Nig) where i was able to learn web development and was part of the LearnFactory team that developed softwares and applications for clients. For the past few months, I've been focusing on interface and web design, but i also love doing graphics design.
I try to practice these skills and interest in my personal projects which helps me to develop and improve as a web developer
